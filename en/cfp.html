Call for Paper

Become a speaker at Hacktrick'18

For an application, along with the speaker's name, institution or school, phone number, e-mail address, short biography, subject matter of the talk, and conference information you have previously attended, you can send the abstract with the PDF form to cfp@hacktrickconf.com.

The information will only be used in communication for the Hacktrick committee with speaker candidate. It will not be shared with third parties.

Note that the content is technically sufficient. Presentations describing direct commercial products will not be accepted.

Presentation Topics

Other topics that may be involved in cyber security and computer science will also be assessed.

  • Cyber Intelligence
  • Hardware Security
  • Physical Security
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Malwares and Antiviruses
  • Weaknesses Disclosure
  • IOT Security
  • Social Engineering
  • Digital Forensics
  • Cryptography
  • Steganography
  • Current Cyber Happenings
  • Hackers and Groups
  • Web Application Security
  • Mobile Application Security
  • Security of Biometric Techologies

Hacktrick 18 will be held on 11-12-13 May 2018 in cooperation with the Information and Communication Technologies Authority and the Octosec Cyber Security Community on the ICTA premises in Ankara.
